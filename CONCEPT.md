# Concept 4: Story-Driven

## Design Philosophy
**סיפור שמתחבר.** This design walks through a relatable customer journey. It's like reading a story about someone just like you, who found the solution. Creates emotional connection through narrative.

## Key Features
- **Narrative structure**: Beginning (problem), Middle (discovery), End (transformation)
- **Character-driven**: "Meet David, a business owner just like you"
- **Journey visualization**: Steps showing progression
- **Emotional moments**: Before/after feelings, not just outcomes
- **Testimonial-like storytelling**: Real-feeling stories

## Target Audience
People who connect with stories, want to see themselves in the solution, appreciate understanding over features.

## Visual Style
- Timeline/journey graphics
- Story cards with quotes
- Progress indicators
- Human photography
- Warm, inviting colors

## Hebrew Messaging
- Storytelling: "זה התחיל כש...", "ואז קרה משהו"
- Emotional words: "מתחילה", "הקלה", "שקט"
- Relatable scenarios
