# Concept 2: Problem-Solution

## Design Philosophy
**אמפתיה לפני פתרון.** This design starts by showing deep understanding of the customer's struggles. It feels like a consultation, not a sales pitch. We acknowledge the pain before offering relief.

## Key Features
- **Opens with pain identification**: "Is this you?" style questions
- **Consulting tone**: Like talking to a trusted advisor
- **Step-by-step journey**: Pain → Understanding → Solution → Relief
- **Emotional connection**: Relatable scenarios and language
- **Gentle CTAs**: Invitations, not demands

## Target Audience
Business owners who feel overwhelmed and want someone to understand their situation before pushing a solution.

## Visual Style
- Warmer, softer colors
- Question-based headlines
- Conversation bubbles or quote styling
- Gentle transitions between sections
- Photography of people (relatable)

## Hebrew Messaging
- Questions: "מרגיש ככה?", "נמאס לך מ...?"
- Understanding: "אנחנו מבינים", "בדיוק בשביל זה"
- Soft, conversational tone
