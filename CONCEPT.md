# Concept 3: Minimalist Premium

## Design Philosophy
**פחות זה יותר.** This design breathes. Generous whitespace, minimal text, maximum impact. Feels exclusive, curated, high-end. Every word earns its place.

## Slogans
- Primary: "זמן הוא הנכס היקר ביותר שלך" (Time is Your Most Valuable Asset)
- Secondary: "אנחנו מחזירים לך אותו" (We Return It to You)
- CTA: "בואו נדבר" (Let's Talk)

## Design Approach
- **Maximum whitespace:** Elements have room to breathe
- **Minimal text:** 50% fewer words than other concepts
- **Premium typography:** Large, elegant fonts
- **Single focus per section:** One message, one CTA
- **Subtle animations:** Refined, not flashy

## Typography Scale
- Display numbers: 7xl - 9xl, font-extralight
- Headlines: 4xl - 6xl, font-light
- Body: lg, font-light
- Everything: Light weights, generous spacing

## Color Palette
- Black / White / Stone
- Green accent (minimal)
- High contrast for impact

## Interactive Elements
1. **Subtle scroll indicators:** Fade lines
2. **Minimal hover states:** Scale + opacity
3. **Smooth reveals:** Intersection Observer

## Component Approach
- Remove all unnecessary elements
- Single-column layouts where possible
- Text-center for most sections
- No decorative borders/shadows (flat design)
- Typography as decoration
